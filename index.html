Luxury and Ayurveda

A unique combination of Ayurveda and Luxury living. our artisanal incense is irresistibly satisfying.

Therapeutic Grade

Every collection has been formulated with therapeutic benefits keeping the original masala technique intact.

Sustainability

All our products and packaging supplies are 100% plant-based. recyclable and plastic-free. Made in India by a team of local women artisans. HMS is a child labour free company.

Legacy

HMS family based in Pune since 1860 have been passing the secret recipes and wisdom to their coming generations consistently and effectiverly.

Ingredients

Crafted with our ancient secret recipe having Ayurvedic herbs, natural gums, plant resins, sun-dried flowers, botanical extracts and aromatic essential oils.

Process

At first, select ingredients are hand-blended by the artisans to form a dough.

The dough is then hand-rolled onto the bamboo sticks.

The sticks are sun-dried to keep the fragrance intact. Lastly, only the flawless ones are hand-picked and hand-packed carefully so that they stay fresh for long.

Limited Produce

All our produce is limited since the supply of ingredients is unpredictable and availability of any specific oil is limited.
