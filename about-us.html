Our Story

What started in 1860 as an ode to our tradition, has flourished into a way of life At HMS, hand-craft is a custom that has been handed down through generations to empower communities to thrive. From sourcing nature's purest ingredients to our ethical practices, we put our soul into every product. We believe in staying true to our culture as we preserve this

Emberacing Our Soil

As a brand, we delve into ancient Indian wisdom and revive its powerful knowledge flowing through the ages. We trust and disseminate ayurvedic traditions, relying on them to create therapeutic incense sticks for a blissful experience.

Uplifting the Local

Working alongside local harvesters has been an invaluable part of the HMS legacy. Since our inception, we have worked towards empowering small businesses. By sourcing fresh gums, resins, rare oils and barks from premium floral farms, Himalayan forests and African jungles, we help expand the business potential of local harvesters and prevent wastage of produce.

Reviving the Lost Arts

HMS's journey is deeply connected to the country's indigenous economy. Currently, 50 artisans work full-time in dough-making, quality testing and packaging. We passionately collaborate with local businesses, enabling them to expand the scope of their operations and innovations. By sourcing through small vendors and interweaving their story into our products, we strive to position traditional artists and domestic suppliers on the global map.

Standing by our Women

HMS encourages self-reliance and financial independence. 500 women are employed with us in a part-time capacity, making incense sticks for added income. Our free training sessions and provision of hand-rolling kits for skilled women have opened up many economic opportunities for them to pursue.

Crafting a Devine Experience

HMS incense sticks cater to a niche clientele, attempting to infuse one's surroundings with tranquillity We foster treasured relationships with temples, ashrams, trusts and international yoga schools. It isn't about corporatisation. It is about connecting to your soul.

Our Special Touch

The HMS ethos marries a love for cultural traditions and the environment with a drive to be a sustainable, community-oriented enterprise. Ethical business practices and superior sourcing is of central importance. We produce plastic-free, plant-based incense tailored for meditative practices and air purification. Instead of wood and commercial perfume, we use bamboo for its unique regenerative quality and rare natural ingredients for its therapeutic benefits. Our closely-knit family consists of an ever-growing network of artisans who we not only employ in varying capacities but actively mentor to help achieve self-employment. As an organisation, we profoundly value the growth of the local enterprise. For that reason, we are planning to expand our hand-rolling units across the country and bring in more and more people in the conversion process of turning our dough into sticks. By re-energising traditional underutilised factories, our manufacturing facilities will employ thousands who have lost their livelihood after machines took over.



The Path For Tomorrow

At HMS, the future is about conceptualising and creating the finest handcrafted products that add value to the lives of our patrons and every person involved in the manufacturing process. We look forward to delivering unique yet traditional experiences for our clients across the globe while back home, actively collaborating with local businesses and becoming a propellor for their growth. We dream of transforming traditional household objects into a luxury market that celebrates heritage and creativity
